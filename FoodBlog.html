Time to Review!

  • Pierogi are often compared to dumplings, they are dough filled delicacies that are very popular in Poland.
  • To make them, you prepare dough and wrap it around an assortment of fillings, such as fruit, meat, and potatoes.
  • They are a bit too big to be called bite size, but they are around a similar size as a dumpling.
  • Pierogi take a while to make, but when you have the entire family helping out they become more manageable!
